# About Me
I'm Saimona. I am engineering financial architectures through a brutal Quantitative Finance roadmap, marrying absolute mathematical foundations like Real Analysis and Stochastic Calculus with unforgiving Machine Learning predictive models. My ambitions extend far beyond mere Scopus Q1 publications; I am relentlessly focused on extracting Alpha amidst extreme market volatility by exploiting Market Microstructure and Latency Arbitrage down to the millisecond. To achieve this dominance, I am building a Tech-dominant HFT (High-Frequency Trading) ecosystem utilizing Low-Latency Architecture and Parallelization. I execute cutting-edge Deep Learning architectures driven by Transformer algorithms—shattering the limitations of conventional sequential models to dissect market anomalies—alongside Reinforcement Learning that is mercilessly punished through Walk-Forward Validation to annihilate lookahead bias. By calculating Tail Risk with surgical precision to withstand the impact of Black Swan events, the distributed predictive systems I design are built for one absolute purpose: to disrupt and compete head-to-head against the ferocious algorithmic standards of elite institutions like Citadel, Jane Street, and Renaissance Technologies..
